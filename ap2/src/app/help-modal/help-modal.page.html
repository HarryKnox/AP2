Help Page

Sanus is a fitness application aiming to motivate its users to become healthier and happier.


Upon upload of an exercise the tracker will automatically update.

Tracker Settings :
• Easier = 75 minutes
• Medium = 150 minutes (default setting)
• Harder = 300 minutes

150 minutes is the NHS recommended weekly amount of exercise for a healthy adult.


Upon upload of an exercise you will earn points.

Earning Points :
• 10 minutes of exercise earns you 5 points
• 1 kilometre of distance earns you 5 points

Coming Soon!
• Earn points for leaderboard positions
• Earn points for completing your goals
• Earn points for milestones


Currently the shop is under maintenance and not yet fully functional.

In the future, you will be able to spend your points to purchase cool prizes!


The privacy setting allows you to appear for your eyes only.

Private :
Only you can see your posts, profile page and leaderboard position.

Public :
All users can see your posts, profile page and leaderboard position.
